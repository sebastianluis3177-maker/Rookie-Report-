About Rookie Report

Rookie Report is an independent sports media website focused on NBA, WNBA, soccer, trades, free agency, player analysis, and breaking news.

Our mission is to provide accurate reporting, thoughtful analysis, and engaging sports coverage for fans around the world. We built our trackers and rankings because we were tired of digging through a dozen tabs to find the same information — so we put it all in one place, updated the way we'd want it updated if we were the ones reading it.
